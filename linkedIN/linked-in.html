Life has been so rewarding but so painful, lately. For the past 2 years me and my team have been releasing 4 podcasts a week. It was one of the best decisions for my career, but there is a part of me that has begun cracking from the inside. At 22 I thought with age life would get easier. The more you hustle early on, the easier it will get. And yes, it's true to some extent. Some aspects of my life have definitely become smoother. But, even when you're doing something you love, it doesn't mean it won't be a challenging journey. When I spoke about this with Gauranga Das Sir on the podcast, he had the perfect metaphor to help me put things into perspective. “When you do something you really like, it’s like drinking hot sugarcane juice. Sugarcane juice is sweet so you love having it, but it’s hot so it burns your throat.” Just because you love doing something, does not mean the process won’t test you. He went on to explain that change is the root cause of pain, fear and anxiety. We are afraid of the change in identities. That’s the only thing that evolves when situations/circumstances change. When you lose a job the fear stems from the change in identity of being an employee to not being one. Similarly, experiences like heartbreak, losing loved ones, moving to different countries, or forming new friendships while leaving old ones behind—these all involve changes in our identities. I want you to process this information and let it seep in. If you’re exploring spirituality or experimenting with different belief systems, this podcast is a must watch! #career #change #job #beerbiceps …see more
